> HOW TO FILL THIS IN (for the client):

> - Keep each answer short and self-contained (2–5 sentences) — write it as if
>   you're replying to a customer, because that's exactly what happens.
> - Phrase the question the way a real visitor would ask it ("How much does a TV

Q: What services does Orion Media offer?
A: We offer four core services: TV media buying (planning and negotiating
airtime), TV commercial production (from concept to final broadcast master),
integrated media campaigns across broadcast, digital and out-of-home, and
advertisement design and production. We're a full-service agency, so we can
handle a campaign end to end.

Q: Where is Orion Media based?
A: We're headquartered in Abu Dhabi, UAE, and we work across the Emirates and
the wider region.

Q: Who are your typical clients?
A: We work with government entities, national broadcasters, and regional brands
across the UAE. Our partners have included Emirates News Agency (WAM), Dubai
Culture, and several federal and government bodies.

Q: How much does a TV campaign cost?
A: Pricing depends on the scope — airtime budget, production complexity, and
campaign length all factor in. We put together a tailored quote for each
client. Share a few details about your goals and timeline and our team will
prepare pricing for you.

Q: How do I get a quote or start a project?
A: Email us at hello@orionilam.com with a short description of what you need —
the brand, the broadcast window, or the commercial you'd like produced — and
our team will follow up to scope it and send a quote.

Q: How long does it take to produce a TV commercial?
A: Timelines vary by complexity, so we confirm a schedule once we understand
the scope. For an accurate timeline, reach out at hello@orionilam.com and we'll
map it out with you.

Q: Do you buy airtime on specific channels?
A: Yes — we plan and negotiate airtime across national and regional broadcast
networks, choosing placements based on real audience data to reach the right
viewers.

Q: How can I contact Orion Media?
A: You can reach the team directly at hello@orionilam.com. We're based in Abu
Dhabi, UAE.
